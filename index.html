✨ Why Choose Currtra Premium?

• Complete Clarity: Track all your accounts, income, and expenses in one elegant dashboard.
• Intelligent Analytics: Understand your spending habits with intuitive charts and breakdowns.
• Seamless Goals: Plan for the future by allocating funds towards your savings goals effortlessly.

🔒 Privacy & Convenience First

• Auto Drafts: Automatically drafts transactions by listening to device notifications.
• 100% Offline Compatible: Fully functional without an internet connection.
• No Privacy Leaks: Your data stays locally with you. No trackers, no shared data.
• Daily Backups: Optional daily back-ups to your own Google Drive space. We do not track or have access to it.
